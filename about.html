I suppose a formal introduction is appropriate. I'm Madison Ilia. I live in the middle of nowhere town, where the sun shines once a week if we're lucky, we have more rocks than grass, and the trees have taken over; but it's ok we hold our own when we need to cut it. I talk too much, type even faster, laugh when I shouldn't, drink too much coffee or Rasa (wearerasa.com), and I'm allergic to red food coloring. The reaction is . . . . more talking, faster walking, an overload of writing, and an unattainable amount of energy. If energy were sellable, I wouldn't need my current day/night/overnight job (a little rotating shift humor).

I love Jesus and strive to be the best version of myself every day; some days are better than others.

I'm a legit mom, a dog mom, a horse mom, a sister, a daughter, an energetic individual, and a writer that has many words with a shortage of hours in a day to write them all down. A police dispatcher most days and a sane person every once in a while. A licensed massage therapist, when it suits me. And lastly, a book nerd. What writer do you know that doesn't equally enjoy reading? One might say I have a lot of interests that are scattered all over the place, can't say I would correct them. I like to embrace life and experience as much of this beautiful world God created for us as I can!
